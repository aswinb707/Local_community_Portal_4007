Help & Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for an event?

Go to the “Register for an Event” section on the homepage. Fill out your name, email, select an event, and click Submit. A confirmation message will appear below the form.

2. How to find nearby events?

Scroll to the "Locate Nearby Events" section and click the Find Nearby Events button. Allow location access when prompted. Events within 4km will be listed.

3. Can I save my preferred event type?

Yes! Use the "Save Preferred Event Type" dropdown. Your choice will be remembered even after refreshing the page. You can clear it anytime using the clear button.

4. How to submit feedback?

Visit the “We Value Your Feedback” section. Enter your phone number, select the event you attended, write your thoughts, and click Send Feedback. Your input helps us improve!

5. Need more assistance?

If your query isn’t answered here, please contact us via the Help Contact section of the portal or email your local council office.

6. Accessibility & Browser Support

The portal is mobile-friendly and works on major modern browsers. If you experience issues, ensure your browser is up to date.
